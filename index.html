About Greenwood University

Established in 1950, we've been committed to academic excellence and student success

Our Mission & Vision

Greenwood University is dedicated to creating and disseminating knowledge through research and teaching. We aim to develop leaders who will make a difference globally.

Our campus provides a nurturing environment where students can explore their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and prepare for successful careers.

  • World-class faculty with industry experience
  • State-of-the-art facilities and laboratories
  • Strong industry partnerships for internships
  • Diverse student community from 50+ countries
